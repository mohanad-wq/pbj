P&J Recipe

How to make a classic Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Slices of white bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons of grape jelly or strawberry jam

Instructions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  3. Put the two slices together to make a sandwich.
  4. Your sandwich is ready to enjoy!
